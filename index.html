COLOR

This changes the hue of the icons, window headers, titles and the body of the TamaNOTchi. I tried to make as many steps as possible for maximum cutomization, but you may notice that the step between Yellow and Green is... a bit big, rough. This is because if I tried to shift the hue less, the darker values looked brown, which combined with a yellowish green looked like vomit. Ew. I'm not even leaving that as an option.
