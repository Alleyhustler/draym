Some browsers such as Opera have and RSS reader integrated (even tho I don't like it because it redirects you instead of letting you read the full content of the entry when clicking on it, but ok) (there's an extension called Smart RSS that makes it better), other browsers such as MS Edge don't.

There are online RSS readers, I personally use Feeder (there's the link to my feed there btw), because the free plan is enough for me and doesn't have ads like many others have.

If your browser doesn't read RSS feeds AND you want to download one without having to make an account, I recommend downloading Fluent Reader JUST because it's open source. It's a bit meh, and there are better alternatives, but they're either paid or have ads (ew, (late stage) capitalism).
